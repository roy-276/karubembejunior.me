About Author

Karubembe Junior is holds a bachelor's degree in Economics and Statistics from Kenya's Laikipia University. He is a young leader who formerly served as the secretary general of the Laikipia University Student Union. He also served on the executive board of the Christian Union as Treasurer. He also serves as the youth governor for Meru County in the Youth Governors national wing.

Author

About me 2

Karubembe Junior is the creator of We Choose You, a non-profit organization that he utilizes to give back to the community and engage with young people. He has two novels that have been published and six manuscripts that have yet to be published. He is also a young mentor who has worked with a number of young people in high schools, elementary schools, churches, and universities.
